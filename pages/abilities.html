Abilities

Name Description
Power Transder (Q) Viktor blasts an enemy with a laser, dealing magic damage and granting a shield that lasts for 2.5 seconds.
Gravity Field (W) Viktor creates a gravitational field that slows all enemies within it and interrupts their dashes, channels, and jumps. The field lasts for 4 seconds.
Death Ray (E) Viktor fires a laser beam that deals magic damage to all enemies in its path. After a brief delay, the laser detonates, dealing additional damage in a large area.
Chaos Storm (R) Viktor deploys a hovering device that emits a large storm of energy. The storm deals magic damage every 0.5 seconds to all enemies within it for 7 seconds.
